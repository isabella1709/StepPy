Welcome to StepPy
StepPy provides step-by-step solutions designed to simplify, generalize, and speed up complex mathematical calculations. Developed during our third semester of college in the Physical Phenomena Modeling course with professor Frank Alcantara, the calculator's algorithm currently only solves limit calculations. However, we continually expand as we progress through the course.
About us
We are a trio of Computer Science students currently enrolled at the Pontifícia Universidade Católica do Paraná (PUCPR). Our team consists of Isabella Vanderlinde Berkembrock, Lucca Fabricio Magalhães and Vitor Nicoletti. We share a common interest for tackling computational challenges and providing solutions and tools designed to facilitate our freshmen’s lives when enrolling into college. We are dedicated to continuously learning and evolving as developers; therefore, for more information, please visit our GitHub, which is linked in the Documentation section of the menu.
